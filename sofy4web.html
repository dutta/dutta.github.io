My Work


Led the development of the automatic website testing bot, Sofy. The goal of Sofy is to replace human testers for both websites and mobile apps within the next 3-5 years. Sofy for websites, currently, given a website URL can navigate the website, log in and out, and detect dynamic content changes. Developers can train Sofy to perform the tests in the manner they would like and all of the training from each of the developers contributes to Sofy learning even more.
